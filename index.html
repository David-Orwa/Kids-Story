👽 A Visitor from the Stars

That night, Milo stared at the bright moon. "I wish I could bounce on you like a trampoline," he whispered.

Suddenly—ZAP!—a flash of light lit his room. A tiny alien in a bubble helmet floated in!

“Don’t panic!” said the alien. “I’m Zuzu from Moon City. We’re holding a Moon Bounce-Off. We need Earth’s best jumper!”

Milo’s jaw dropped. “You picked me?”

Zuzu handed him glowing boots. “Put these on. Time to bounce!”

🏆 The Great Moon Jump

Milo and Zuzu blasted off! When they landed, aliens from all over the galaxy were bouncing high into the sky!

Milo ran and leapt—he spun, flipped, and even drew a smiley face in the air with sparkles!

The crowd went wild. “Earth kid! Earth kid!” they cheered.

The Moon Judges clapped. “Winner: Milo!” they announced. He was awarded a glowing trophy and a backpack of moon cookies.

Back home in bed, Milo looked down. The boots were still on his feet. Maybe... just maybe... it wasn’t a dream. 🌙
